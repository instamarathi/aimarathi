Presentations & Design

Topic सांगा, complete presentation तयार होते. Slides, design, content — सगळं automatic.

Tool Comparison

Tool Free / Paid Key Notes
Gamma Free tier (10 credits) + Paid ($8/mo) सगळ्यात best AI presentation tool. Topic सांगा, full deck तयार. Design, content, images — सगळं automatic. Export to PPT/PDF.
Canva AI (Magic Design) Free tier + Pro ($13/mo) Canva चं Magic Design feature. Templates खूप आहेत. Drag-and-drop editing सोपं. Team collaboration built-in.
Google Slides + Gemini Free Gemini ला prompt द्या presentation outline साठी, मग Google Slides मध्ये बनवा. पूर्ण free workflow. Collaboration सोपं.

कुठून सुरुवात करायची?

Gamma try करा — topic टाका, 30 seconds मध्ये professional presentation तयार. Free credits मध्ये 10 presentations बनतात. पूर्ण free हवं असेल तर Gemini + Google Slides combo वापरा.

Copy-Paste Prompts

Business Pitch Deck (Gamma साठी)

Create a 10-slide pitch deck for a Pune-based organic food delivery startup called "शेतकरी ते थेट". Include: problem statement, solution, market size in India, business model, competitive advantage, team slide, financial projections, and call to action. Modern, clean design with green and earth tones.

College Presentation (Gamma साठी)

Create an 8-slide educational presentation on "History of Maratha Empire" for a college history class. Include key battles, major rulers from Shivaji Maharaj to Peshwas, timeline, territorial maps description, and legacy. Use a royal theme with saffron and gold accents.

Training Slides (Gemini prompt)

मला एक 12-slide training presentation चा outline तयार करून दे. Topic: "Small Business साठी Digital Marketing Basics". प्रत्येक slide चं title, 3-4 bullet points, आणि speaker notes लिहा. Audience: small shop owners जे digital marketing ला नवीन आहेत. मराठीत लिहा पण technical terms English मध्ये ठेवा.

Workshop Presentation (Gamma साठी)

Create a 15-slide workshop presentation on "AI Tools for Students". Cover: what is AI, free AI tools list, how to use ChatGPT for studies, AI for writing assignments, AI for research, AI image generation, ethics of AI use in education, and hands-on exercise slides. Colorful, engaging design suitable for college students.

Presentation tips

1. Gamma मध्ये "Generate" नंतर edit करा — AI चा first draft चांगला असतो, पण तुमचा specific data, numbers, आणि examples add करा.

2. कमी text, जास्त visuals — प्रत्येक slide वर 3-5 bullet points पुरेसे. बाकी बोलताना सांगा.

3. Export करा — Gamma वरून PowerPoint मध्ये export करता येतं, म्हणजे offline पण present करता येतं.
