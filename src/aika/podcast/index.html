Podcast बनवा

AI वापरून podcast बनवणं आता खूप सोपं झालंय. Script लिहायला, editing करायला, show notes बनवायला, आणि अगदी AI hosts ची discussion बनवायला — सगळ्यासाठी tools आहेत. एकट्याने पण professional podcast चालवता येतो.

Tool Comparison

Tool Free / Paid Key Notes
NotebookLM (Google) Free Documents upload करा, AI दोन hosts ची natural discussion बनवतो (Audio Overview feature). English मध्ये excellent. तुमचे notes, articles, PDFs — कशावरही podcast style conversation बनतो.
Gemini Free Podcast scripts लिहायला best. Episode planning, show notes, guest questions — सगळं Gemini ने होतं. मराठी scripts पण लिहतो.
Descript Free tier (1 hr/month), Hobbyist $24/month Podcast editing magic. Text edit करा — audio automatically edit होतो. "Um", "uh" automatically काढतो. Filler word removal, studio sound — सगळं built-in.

Copy-Paste Prompts

NotebookLM ने AI Podcast बनवा

notebooklm.google.com वर जा → New Notebook → तुमचे documents upload करा (articles, notes, PDFs) → "Audio Overview" वर click करा → Generate.

AI दोन hosts ची 10-15 minute natural discussion बनवतो तुमच्या content वर. English मध्ये output येतो. तुम्ही customization instructions पण देऊ शकता, जसे:

"Focus on the practical applications. Keep the tone casual and fun. Explain technical concepts with simple analogies."

Gemini ने Podcast Script लिहा

"Write a 15-minute podcast script in Marathi about [तुमचा topic]. Two hosts — one expert, one curious beginner. Conversational tone, not scripted-sounding. Include:
- A catchy intro hook (30 seconds)
- 3 main points with examples
- A listener question segment
- Outro with next episode teaser
Mix Marathi and English naturally. Add [PAUSE], [LAUGH], [EMPHASIS] cues for recording."

Episode Show Notes Generate करा

Recording झाल्यावर transcript Gemini ला द्या आणि हा prompt वापरा:

"From this podcast transcript, create show notes with: 1) Episode title — catchy, under 60 characters, 2) One-paragraph summary, 3) Key topics with timestamps (estimate based on position in text), 4) Links/resources mentioned, 5) 3 social media posts to promote this episode — one for Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn. Write in the same language as the transcript."

Season Planning — 10 Episodes Plan करा

"I'm starting a Marathi podcast about [तुमचा broad topic]. Plan 10 episodes for the first season. For each episode give me:
- Episode title (catchy, Marathi-English mix)
- 3-line description
- 3 key talking points
- 1 potential guest suggestion (type of expert, not specific person)
Make sure episodes build on each other. Start simple, go deeper. Mix solo episodes with interview-style episodes."

Tips

AI Podcast Workflow कसा असतो?

  • NotebookLM हा shortcut आहे: तुम्हाला recording करायचं नसेल तर NotebookLM मध्ये documents टाका — AI पूर्ण podcast बनवतो. Blog posts चे podcast episodes बनवायला perfect.
  • Script + Record + Edit: Gemini ने script लिहा → स्वतः record करा (phone वर पण चालतो) → Descript मध्ये edit करा. Professional output मिळतो.
  • Descript चा magic: Recording मध्ये चुकलं तर Descript मध्ये text edit करा — audio automatically cut होतो. "Um" आणि filler words एका click ने निघतात.
  • Consistency ठेवा: Intro आणि outro same ठेवा प्रत्येक episode ला. AI ने एकदा template बनवून घ्या, reuse करा.
  • Free मध्ये सुरू करा: NotebookLM (free) + Gemini (free) + phone recording + Descript free tier — पहिले 10 episodes ला पैसे लागत नाहीत.
