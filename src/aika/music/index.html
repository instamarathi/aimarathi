Copy-Paste Prompts

लावणी Style Song

Write a lavani-style Marathi song about the joy of monsoon arriving in the Sahyadri mountains. Upbeat tempo, traditional instruments like dholki and tuntuni. Female vocals. Lyrics in Marathi Devanagari script.

Ganpati Bappa Song

Create an energetic Ganpati festival song in Marathi. Include dhol-tasha beats, celebratory mood. Lyrics about welcoming Bappa home. Chorus should be catchy and easy to sing along. Male group vocals.

Romantic Marathi Song

Compose a soft romantic Marathi song, natya sangeet inspired. Piano and flute accompaniment. Male vocals, slow tempo. Theme: first rain and memories of someone special. Lyrics in Marathi.

Marathi Rap / Hip-Hop

Write a Marathi hip-hop track about Mumbai local train life. Fast-paced beat, modern production. Mix Marathi and English lyrics. Theme: daily hustle, dreams, and city energy. Male rapper voice.

Tips

चांगले results कसे मिळवायचे?

  • Genre specify करा: "lavani", "natya sangeet", "Bollywood pop" — जितकं specific तितकं चांगलं.
  • Mood सांगा: "energetic", "melancholic", "devotional" — AI ला mood कळला तर music match होतो.
  • Instruments सांगा: "tabla, sitar, flute" किंवा "electronic, synth" — तुम्हाला हवा तो sound मिळेल.
  • Suno चा Custom Mode वापरा: lyrics स्वतः लिहा आणि style of music separately सांगा — यामुळे best control मिळतो.
  • Short clips बनवा आधी: पूर्ण song बनवण्यापूर्वी 30-second clip बनवून बघा. आवडलं तर extend करा.
  • Paid plan चा विचार करा: Free tier मध्ये दिवसाला 5 songs ची limit आहे — music seriously explore करायचं असेल तर Suno Pro ($10/mo) ने unlimited songs मिळतात, मग मनसोक्त experiment करता येतो.
